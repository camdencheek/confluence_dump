Eligibility Review

Once a user has submitted the initial 'Screener' form the nursing staff will be notified and began an eligibility review. Once this review is completed the applicant will receive an email stating rather they have been approved or not. If approved the user will be informed that their consent form is ready to be signed and will populate on their page. After the consent is signed and approved, the user will receive another email indicating additional forms (enrollment packet) have been added to their home page. These forms must be completed before moving forward in the study.

Consent Form

Once your Screener is completed and approved you will see an additional form for consent populate on your home page. The system will also send an email informing you that your Consent form needs to be signed.
  1. Click the Consent Icon.
  2.  Scroll through the entire form and answer the three (3) questions at the bottom of the screen. The 'Consent' option will be disabled until the questions have all been answered.
  3.  Click the 'Consent' icon.
  4. The sign screen will populate. Enter your password to verify your identity and click the 'Sign' option.
  5. You will see a Consent form submitted successfully message populate. Click the 'OK' option.

Additional Forms

Once your Consent form is signed and approved you will see additional forms (enrollment packet) populate on your home page. The system will also send an email informing you that your Enrollment Packet is ready to be completed. The initial subscriber and first eligible participant for the study is known as the "Proband". If the "Proband" isn't of age or doesn't know all the relevant information they may have a "Proxy". The "Proxy" is a legal representative for the "Proband". Once the Proband has been approved a consent form will be added to their home page. Proband/Proxy Screener Completion

Family History Questionnaire (FHQ)

The Proband will need to submit the FHQ form.
  1. Click on the FHQ icon to open the form.
  2. Based of the information provided in the "Screener", the Research Participant, Date of Birth and Biological Gender will automatically populate. The National Origin/Ancestry question is optional. Verify the information and click the 'Save & Next' option.
  3. This form is broken out into five (5) sections: Demographic Information, Family Information, Spouse/Partner/Child, Family Member Information, Final Information. There is a navigation bar on the left-hand-side so that you can go back-and-forth between each section. Read all of the information thoroughly and answer all questions to the best of your ability.
  4. When the form is completed you will see checks next to each section. You will have the option to go 'Back' to a previous section, 'Save' your work, or 'Submit' your form.
  5. Click the green 'Submit' option when the form is complete.
  6. You will see a FHQ Questionnaire form submitted successfully message populate. Click the 'OK' button.

Medical Research Record (MRR) Consent

All study participants must complete and submit a MRR Consent Form.
  1. Click on the MRR icon.
  2. Scroll through the content. If you are a Proxy for the participant, please check the appropriate response. If you have medical records under a different name, please provide it in the space given. The 'Consent' option will be disabled until the mandatory field is selected. Click the 'Consent' button.
  3. The sign screen will populate. Enter your password to verify your identity and click the 'Sign' option.
  4. You will see a MRR Consent form submitted successfully message populate. Click the 'OK' option.

Individual Information Questionnaire (IIQ) Consent

Additional family members added to the study by the Proband participant must complete and submit a IIQ Form. We will ask you to provide some specific information about aspects of your health that might relate to your own risk of cancer, as well as information about screening tests and procedures you may have had.
  1. Click on the IIQ icon to open the form.
  2. The first question in the Introduction section will ask "are you filling this questionnaire out for someone else?" If the answer is 'No' click the 'Save & Next' option to continue. If the answer is 'Yes' you will be asked additional questions regarding the aforementioned person before continuing.
  3. This form is broken out into sixteen (16) sections: Introduction, Demographic Information, Biopsy(ies) & Cancer(s), Surgery(ies), Medications and Supplements, Health Practices, Screening Tests, Tobacco Use, Alcohol Use, Physical Development, Beliefs about Cancer Risk, Distress in the Past 7 Days,Feelings and Thoughts During the Last Month, Coping Mechanisms, Personal Support and Final Information. There is a navigation bar on the left-hand-side so that you can go back-and-forth between each section. Read all of the information thoroughly and answer all questions to the best of your ability.
4. When the form is completed you will see checks next to each section. You will have the option to go 'Back' to a previous section, 'Save' your work, or 'Submit' your form.5. Click the green 'Submit' button when the form is complete. Note: If the additional member is a "Female" there will be additional questions in the IIQ. If the additional member is under the age of 17 they are considered a "Minor" and will need a "Proxy" to complete the form. If the member is under the age of 14 the IIQ will be adjusted to a Pediatric Information Questionnaire (PIQ). This is a shorter version of the IIQ with some questions omitted.

Uploading Additional Information (Proband)

After the participant has completed all the required forms, the nursing staff may contact them for clarification and additional information. This could include specific documentation such as medical records. To upload these documents to your file follow the steps below:
  1. Scan a copy of the documentation required and save it to the laptop or tablet you are using.
  2. From the home page click on the 'Upload Attachment' icon.
  3. The "Additional Document" screen will populate with your name automatically visible as the "Research Participant". From the drop-down arrow select the "File Category" for the document and click the 'Done' option. Next click the 'Browse' option.
  4. Click the 'Browse' option.
  5. Find the document you wish to upload and select it. If you would like to rename the file you can do so in the "Save As" block. Verify the information is correct and click the 'Upload' option.
  6. At this point you will have the option to remove the file if you uploaded the wrong document. If you are satisfied with the document you uploaded click the 'Done' option.
  7. You will then see a message stating "File has been uploaded successfully".

Uploading Additional Information (Proxy)

  1. Scan a copy of the documentation required and save it to the laptop or tablet you are using.
  2. From your Home page you will see "Requested Enrollment Documents" over the Upload Attachment icon. Click the 'Upload' Option under Proxies.
  3. The "Additional Document" screen will populate with your name automatically visible as the "Research Participant". From the drop-down arrow select the "File Category" for the document and click the 'Done' option. The name(s) of the research participants you are responsible will be listed. If you are just responsible for one person that will be the only name listed. Choose the participant you are uploading the file for.
  4. Click the 'Browse' option.
  5. Find the document you wish to upload and select it. You will then see the name of the participant you selected and the the name of the document you want to upload in the boxes. If you would like to rename the file you can do so in the "Save As" block. Verify the information is correct and click the 'Upload' option.
  6. At this point you will have the option to remove the file if you uploaded the wrong document. If you are satisfied with the document you uploaded click the 'Done' option.
  7. You will then see a message stating "File has been uploaded successfully".Note: If you are a Proxy and you have various forms that have been previously loaded to your account. You have the option to upload those files into the record of specific participants by clicking the 'Upload' option as well.
