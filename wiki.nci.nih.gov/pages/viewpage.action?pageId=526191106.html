For your convenience, you can find a recording of a Training Opportunities session previously hosted by the program. Please find a brief overview (with links) below:
  • Postdocs are usually up to five years at the NCI (sometimes longer depending on your field and goals). Things are still adjusting following COVID, and there still isn't a consensus on what the "new normal" will be. As such, everyone's experiences may be different for the next year or so.
  • Stipends start at around $64,000 (which may vary depending on the division), and increase each year based as you gain additional research experience.
  • Health insurance is provided to you at no cost, and includes medical, prescription, vision, and dental (provided by FAES Health Insurance ). However, if you prefer your own health insurance, you can provide evidence of that instead.
  • There are a number of fellow groups, as well as career and professional development resources, that can be found by visiting our Resources for Trainees website .
GSRP TIMELINEFollowing acceptance, you will need to do two things: 1) identify NCI investigators with whom you would like to meet with and 2) arrange your travel with the logistics coordinator. You can find an example agenda under Preparing for the Conference , however, please note that there is a welcome social (with lite fare) on the day of arrival, a full day of GSRP activities on Day 1, and a (mostly) full day for you to arrange meetings with investigators at the campus of your choosing on Day 2.
