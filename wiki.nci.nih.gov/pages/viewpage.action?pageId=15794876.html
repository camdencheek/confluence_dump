National Cancer Institute Center for Bioinformatics NCI Protege 1.3 and patch Release Notes 09 04 2009

Product Changes

Items marked New Feature: can be found in the GForge project Feature Requests tracker group. Items marked Bug: can be found in the Bugs tracker group. The major change in this release is the addition of "database inclusion" which enables other terminologies to be imported and "federated". Another major change is workflow additions that enable proposals to be retrieved from the BiomedGT wiki.
