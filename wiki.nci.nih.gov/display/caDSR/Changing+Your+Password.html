If you know your current caDSR password, you can change it:
  1. Log in as described in Logging In . The home page appears.
  2. Select Personalize > Change Password. The Change Password page appears.
  3. Type your current password.
  4. Type your new password and retype it to confirm. For password rules and guidelines, refer to About Passwords .
  5. Click Save. The home page reappears with a message at the top confirming the password change.
