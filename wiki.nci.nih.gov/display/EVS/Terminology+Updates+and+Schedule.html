This page identifies the vocabulary versions currently in production and dates of upcoming version updates. Note, the dates presented are approximate. They can be impacted by user request and by system maintenance schedules. Past versions of the NCI Thesaurus are archived and available for download . The NCI Metathesaurus is available for download to users with UMLS licenses .
