Administrators are able to track issues and change the status of issues.
  1. Log in to the challenge management system.You must log in to see the Report Issue and Issue Status options.The admin features appear.
  2. Click Issue Status to see a list of all issues, their details, and their status. An issue's status can be one of the following status types:
    • Open
    • Closed
    • Verified
    • Reopen
    • Waiting for User Input
    • Support team Working
