This page explains how to log an issue and assumes that you are not an administrator.
  1. On the Competition page, log in to access the Report Issue tab and log a report.The Report Issue tab does not appear until you log in.
  2. Click Report Issue. The Report Issue page appears.
  3. Enter a title and content for your issue and click Submit. The competition organizers receive your issue and the details you entered appear on the Issue status tab.
