Service Taxonomies We review the definitions and decide to use this week to think about approaches on how to map the con op requirements to these four service taxonomies: - Business/Process - Arbitrarily complex services that utilize the other three service types to carry out business functionsBusiness Capability - Services that provide "business atoms", the data most business processes utilize - Core - Services that provide information components to capability and business services - Infrastructure/Utility - Services that are required or utilized by virtually all other services (most of the SI services will be seen by others as fitting into this category in order for them to build their Business Process Services)
