Review and comment on the Semantic Infrastructure 2.0 Roadmap
  • Review the current PDF version of the Semantic Infrastructure 2.0 Roadmap or the current working version on the wiki .
  • If you have an account on the wiki, you are invited to use the Add Comment link at the bottom of the wiki page on which you want to make a comment, and write your comments there.
  • If you would like a wiki account, please write to the Editor ; provide your name, organization, email and phone number.
  • If you prefer, provide your comments on the Semantic Infrastructure 2.0 Roadmap Community Input Form and send the file to the Editor . Your comments will be posted in your behalf on the relevant wiki page.
  • For additional information, consult the following CBIIT Semantic Infrastructure 2.0 Roadmap Documents :
    • Semantic Infrastructure 2.0 Capabilities and Services Profile (main page included in the SI Roadmap)
    • Standards and Initiatives Related to Semantic Infrastructure 2.0
  • Refer also to caGrid 2.0 and Semantic Infrastructure 2.0 - Reference Frameworks for Development .
Disposition of comments to dateView the comments on each page of the Semantic Infrastructure 2.0 Roadmap to review comments submitted beginning in January and the replies.
