While performing a Simple Search, you can save your choice of Collection(s), Image Modality(ies), and Anatomical Site as a named query. You can then run that query in the future or edit it, if you want to change any of the search criteria. That edited query can either update your original query or become a new query with a new name.The following procedures are associated with saved search queries.

  • Viewing and Editing Saved Queries
  • Viewing Query History
