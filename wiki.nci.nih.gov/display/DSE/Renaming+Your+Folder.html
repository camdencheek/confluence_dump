At any point after you create a folder, you can edit the folder name.
  1. Log in to the CSSI DCC Portal, as described in Logging In to the CSSI DCC Portal .
  2. Select Investigations > Upload. The Upload ISA Archives page appears.
  3. On the Upload ISA Archives page, in the row for the folder of interest, click Open. The folder name appears in the Single Archive Upload section of the page, above the list of investigation versions.
  4. Click the edit icon next to the folder name. The Folder Name field appears.
  5. Enter a unique name for your folder. This name can differ from the title within the ISA archive.
  6. Click Save. The folder name you specified appears in the list of folders at the top of the page, and above the File Upload section.
