Globus is a service that securely enables transfers of large files. To download investigation files from CSSI DCC with Globus:
  • You must have an account with Globus. If you do not already have a Globus account, the system prompts you to create one when you start the download process.
  • Install Globus Connect Personal as described in the following page: https://www.globus.org/globus-connect-personal
  • Visit the following page and make a note of your Globus ID. (To locate your ID in Globus, click Account and refer to the Identities tab.) https://app.globus.org/account/identities Your Globus ID is in the form of an email address, and it might not be the same email address as that associated with your DCC account.
Now you can download as described in the following pages:
  • Downloading Full Data with Globus
  • Downloading Selected Data with Globus
