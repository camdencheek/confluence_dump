To save search parameters
  1. Log in to the CSSI DCC portal. For instructions, refer to Logging In to the CSSI DCC Portal . Your email address appears in the upper-right corner of the screen.
  2. Perform a search, as described in one of the following sections:
    • Performing a Basic Search
    • Searching by Keyword or Phrase in All Fields
    • Searching by Keyword or Phrase in ISA Tab Fields
    • Searching by Related Terms from Ontologies
  3. Click Save Search. The Saved Search dialog box appears with details about the search you are saving.
  4. Specify a name for this search. Click Save. A message appears indicating that theÂ system has saved your search.
  5. Click Ok.
For instructions on managing your saved searches, refer to Managing Saved Search Parameters .
