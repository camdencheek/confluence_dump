1. Log in to the CSSI DCC Portal, as described in Logging In to the CSSI DCC Portal .
  2. Click your email address in the top navigation bar to open the profile menu.
  3. Select My Groups.The My Groups page appears.
  4. Click Create Group.The Create a Group page appears.
  5. Enter a group name and description (both are required) and then click Submit. The group is now available when assigning group access to investigation data. For instructions, refer to Assigning Group Access to Investigation Data .
