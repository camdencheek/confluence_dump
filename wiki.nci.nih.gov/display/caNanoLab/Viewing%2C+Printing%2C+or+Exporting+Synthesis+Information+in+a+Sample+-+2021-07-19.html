You can view information in an existing sample.
  1. Search for the sample, as described in Searching for Existing Samples . The Sample Search Results page appears.
  2. In the row for the sample you want to view, click View (or Edit, if that option is available to you). The Sample page appears. (If you are logged in and an Edit button is not available, you have read-only access to the data.)
  3. In the Navigation Tree, click Synthesis. The Sample (sample name) Synthesis page appears.
  4. Select the tab for the category of information you want to view (Materials, Functionalization, or Purification), or remain on the default tab (All).
  5. To print the information, click Print at the top right of the page.
  6. To export the information to a spreadsheet, click Export at the top right of the page.
To export all sample information to JSON or XML, refer to Exporting Sample Information to JSON or XML - include .
