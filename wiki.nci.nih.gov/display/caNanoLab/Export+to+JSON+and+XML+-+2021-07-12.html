You can export a sample's general information, composition, characterization, publication, and synthesis information to JSON or XML.To export sample information from the Navigation Tree
  1. From the Navigation Tree, select General Info.
    NAVIGATION TREE
    GENERAL INFO
    COMPOSITION
    CHARACTERIZATION
    PUBLICATION
    SYNTHESIS
  2. Click Export (JSON) or Export (XML).
  3. The system prompts you to download the information and specify a download location.
