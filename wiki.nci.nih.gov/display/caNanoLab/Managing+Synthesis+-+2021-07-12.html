This chapter describes how to manage synthesis information in caNanoLab samples.
  • If you have a read-only account, you can view, print, or export sample information. For details, refer to Viewing, Printing, or Exporting Synthesis Information in a Sample - 2021-07-19 .
  • If you have a curator account associated with an existing sample, you can also add, edit, or delete information in that sample.
