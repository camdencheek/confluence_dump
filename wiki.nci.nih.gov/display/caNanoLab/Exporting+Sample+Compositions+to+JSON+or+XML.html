You can export a sample's General Information, Composition, Characterization, and Publication information to JSON and XML.To export sample information from the Navigation Tree
  1. From the Navigation Tree, select General Info.
    NAVIGATION TREE
    GENERAL INFO
    COMPOSITION
    CHARACTERIZATION
    PUBLICATION
  2. Click Export (JSON) or Export (XML).
  3. The information is downloaded, and you can save it to a location.
