The caNanoLab My Favorites menu option displays samples, protocols, and publications that you bookmark for quick access.To add a protocol to My Favorites
  1. Perform a search.
  2. On the search results, click + Add to Favorites in the Actions column.
    The Added to Favorites message appears in the results row.
  3. Click the My Favorites menu, and the bookmarked protocol is listed under My Protocols.
