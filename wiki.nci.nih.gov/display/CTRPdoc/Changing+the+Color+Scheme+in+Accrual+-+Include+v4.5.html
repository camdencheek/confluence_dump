You can choose a site-wide color scheme to suit your preferences.How to Change the Color Scheme
  1. Visit the CTRP Accrual home page at https://trials.nci.nih.gov .
  2. Log in using your CTRP account and navigate to the CTRP Accrual application.
  3. On the top right corner of any page, click (User Name) > My Account.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the My Account window and select one of the five color schemes.
