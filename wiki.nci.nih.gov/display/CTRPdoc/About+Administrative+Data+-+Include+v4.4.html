You can edit or add most trial-related administrative information except identifiers that the system generates, such as the NCI Trial Identifier. Administrative data for trials comprises the following information:
  • General trial details
  • NCI-specific information
  • Regulatory information
  • Human subject safety
  • Trial IND/IDE
  • Trial status
  • Trial funding
  • Participating sites
  • Collaborators
  • Trial-related documents
