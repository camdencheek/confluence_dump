You can edit or delete trial associations as necessary. When editing an association, you can change the details of associated trials that have not  been registered with the CTRP.  You cannot change the details of trials that have been registered, but you can change the association to a different trial, or create a new association.When you delete an associated trial record, you disassociate it from another trial. The trial record itself is not deleted.How to Edit and Delete Trials
  1. Search for, and select, the trial whose associations you want to edit or delete. For instructions, see Searching for Trials .
  2. On the main menu, click Associated Trials.The Associated Trials page displays one or more associated trial records.
  3. To edit an associated trial, click the Edit icon (pencil).The Add/Edit Trial Association page appears. If you are editing an associated trial that is currently registered in the CTRP, you can change the current association to a different trial that is also registered in the CTRP. You can not change any other aspect of the trial information.If you are editing an associated trial that has not been registered in the CTRP, you can change any aspect of the trial information. Add/Edit Associated Trail for Associations to Trials Registered in the CTRP Add/Edit Associated Trail for Associations to Trials That are not Registered in the CTRP
  4. Do one of the following to edit the associated trial:
    • If the associated trial is currently registered in the CTRP, click Look Up Trial and select the trial from the results list. You are returned to the Associated Trials page automatically.-or-
    • If the associated trial is not currently registered in the CTRP, enter new trial information as per the instructions in Adding and Editing Trial Associations , and then click Save. You are returned to the Associated Trials page automatically.
  5. To disassociate one or more trials, select the check box(es) in the Delete column, and then click Delete. To disassociate all trials, click Select All, and then click Delete.
