How to Add Trial Associations
  1. Search for, and select, the trial you want to associate with other trials. For instructions, see Searching for Trials .
  2. On the main menu, click Associated Trials.The Associated Trials page may display one or more associated trial records.
  3. Click Add.The Add/Edit Associated Trial page appears.
  4. Do one of the following to identify the trial you want to associate:
    • To search for a trial that has been registered in the CTRP, click Look Up Trial, search for the trial, and then select its NCI Trial Identifier link.
    - or -
    • To associate a trial that has not yet been registered in the CTRP, select or enter the information in the fields provided. Fields marked with an asterisk ( * ) are required.
      Field Label Description/Instructions
      Trial Identifier* Type the numeric or alphanumeric identifier.Examples:
      • NCI-2000-00015
      • ECOG-1234
      Inter-Group trials use the lead Group’s trial number.
      Identifier Type* Select the type of identifier you want to search for from the drop-down list. For identifier type descriptions, see the list of identifiers in Searching for Trials .
      Trial Type Select Interventional or Non-Interventional.
      Trial Sub-Type Select Observational or Ancillary-Correlative.
      Official Title Enter, or copy and paste, the name of the clinical trial as it appears in the Protocol document.
  5. Click Save.The trials are associated. You can repeat the steps above to associate other trials.
