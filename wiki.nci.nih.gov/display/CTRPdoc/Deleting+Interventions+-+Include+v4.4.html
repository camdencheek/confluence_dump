When you delete interventions from a trial, you delete them from view, and can not recover them. If you delete a record accidentally, follow the instructions in Adding Interventions  to add it back.How to Delete Interventions
  1. Search for the trial of interest. For instructions, refer to Searching for Trials in PA .
  2. In the search results, click the NCI Trial Identifier link for that trial. The Trial Identification page appears.
  3. On the Trial Identification page, check out the trial. For instructions, refer to Checking In and Checking Out Trials . (This checkout step is optional for Super Abstractors.)
  4. On the Scientific Data menu, click Interventions. The Intervention page appears.
  5. In the Delete column, select the check box for each record you want to delete, then click Delete. To delete all records, click Select All, and then click Delete.
