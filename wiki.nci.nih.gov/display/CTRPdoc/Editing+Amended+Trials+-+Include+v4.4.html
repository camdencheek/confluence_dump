You can edit your amended trial details after you have reviewed them at any time before you submit them to the CTRP.How to Edit Amended Details
  1. Scroll to the bottom of the Review Trial Details page, and click Edit. The Amendment Trial page displays all information you have provided in editable form.
  2. Make changes as necessary and then follow the instructions in Reviewing and Submitting Trial Amendments .
Editing Trial-Related DocumentsThe Edit Trial page lists all documents that were submitted with previous trial submissions. You can view, but not delete these documents. However, you can remove/replace the documents associated with the amendment you are submitting.
