The final step before accepting or rejecting a trial is to validate the trial details.How to Validate Trial Details
  1. Select a trial to validate by following the instructions in Selecting Trials that Require Validation . The Trial Identification page displays the trial details.
  2. On the Trial Identification page, check out the trial. For instructions, refer to Checking In and Checking Out Trials . (This checkout step is optional for Super Abstractors.)
  3. On theÂ Validation menu, click Trial Validation.
  4. In the drop-down lists and text fields, select or enter the appropriate information. The following table describes the fields. An asterisk (*) indicates a required field.
    Field Label Instructions
    Lead Organization Trial ID* (under Other Trial Identifiers) Ensure that the ID matches the one provided in the protocol. Make changes as necessary. Refer to Abstracting Trial Descriptions, Titles, and Identifiers .
    ClinicalTrials.gov Identifier (under Other Trial Identifiers) Refer to Abstracting Trial Descriptions, Titles, and Identifiers .
    Other identifiers (under Other Trial Identifiers) Refer to Abstracting Trial Descriptions, Titles, and Identifiers .
    Official Title* Type or correct the trial title as necessary. You can use a maximum of 4000 characters. Refer to Abstracting Trial Descriptions, Titles, and Identifiers .
    Trial Phase* Refer to Abstracting Interventional Trial Design or Abstracting Non-Interventional Trial Design .
    Is this a Pilot? Refer to Abstracting Interventional Trial Design or Abstracting Non-Interventional Trial Design .
    Primary Purpose* Refer to Abstracting Interventional Trial Design or Abstracting Non-Interventional Trial Design .
    If Primary Purpose is "Other", describe (available if Primary Purpose = Other) Refer to Abstracting Interventional Trial Design or Abstracting Non-Interventional Trial Design .
    Lead Organization* Click Look Up Organization. Refer to Searching for Organizations in PA .
    Principal Investigator* Click Look Up Person. Refer to Searching for Persons in PA .
    Sponsor Refer to Abstracting Sponsors and Responsible Parties .
    Responsible Party Refer to Abstracting Sponsors and Responsible Parties .
    Data Table 4 Funding Category Refer to Abstracting NCI-Specific Information .
    Data Table 4 Funding Source Refer to Abstracting NCI-Specific Information .
    Program Code Refer to Abstracting NCI-Specific Information .
  5. Do one of the following to continue:
    • To save the trial information so that you can continue to validate the trial at a later time, click Save. - or -
    • To put the trial on hold, for example, when you require further information from the submitter, click Place on Hold. Refer to Putting Trial Processing On Hold . - or -
    • To accept or reject the trial, follow the instructions in Accepting and Rejecting Trials .
