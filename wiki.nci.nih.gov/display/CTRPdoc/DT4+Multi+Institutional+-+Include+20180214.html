An indication whether there is more than one Cancer Center participating in the trial, derived as follows:
  • Y (Yes) = There is more than one site (organization) participating in the trial, and these participating sites are not all members of the same Cancer Center (organization family).
  • N (No) = One or more sites are participating in the trial, but all participating sites are members of the same Cancer Center.
Note:  Based on CTRP (rather than ClinicalTrials.gov relationships).
