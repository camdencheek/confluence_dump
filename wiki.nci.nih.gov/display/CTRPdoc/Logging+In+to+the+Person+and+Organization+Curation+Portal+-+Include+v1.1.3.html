How to Log In to the P/O Curation Portal
  1. Open your browser and navigate to the P/O Curation Portal at https://trials.nci.nih.gov/curation .The P/O Curation Portal Login page appears.
  2. Enter your CTRP account username and password in the fields provided.
  3. Click Login. The NCI Shared Person/Organization Curation Portal disclaimer appears.
  4. After you have read the disclaimer, click Accept. The Welcome page displays an expanded menu with links to the curation tools.
Timeout periodThe system logs you out if it detects that you have not used the application for 240 minutes at any point in the curation workflow. The system locks you out after three unsuccessful attempts to log in within 24 hours. In the event that you have been locked out of your account, contact us at ctrp_support@nih.gov . If you have forgotten your password, or if you want to reset it for any reason, refer to Resetting Your Password .
