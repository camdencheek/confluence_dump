The system maintains documents even after abstractors have deleted them.How to Access Deleted Documents
  1. Search for the trial of interest. For instructions, refer to Searching for Trials in PA .
  2. In the search results, click the NCI Trial Identifier link for that trial. The Trial Identification page appears.
  3. On the Trial Overview menu, click Trial History. The Trial History Information page appears.
  4. On theÂ Submissions page, scroll to the bottom of the list of submissions, and then click Show Deleted Documents. All deleted documents are displayed.
  5. To close the Deleted Documents list, click Hide Deleted Documents.
