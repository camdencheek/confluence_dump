How to Delete Site Subject Accrual Counts
  1. Select the study you want to work with by following instructions in Searching for and Selecting Your Trials , and clicking the corresponding NCI Trial Identifier link. The Participating Site Subject Accrual Count page appears.
Deleting all Subjects from a Study
  1. Click Delete All Trial Study Subjects
  2. Select a value from the Reason listbox
  3. Click OK
NOTE:  Be careful when deleting All Trial Study Subjects.  Incorrectly deleted accrual data must be resubmitted by the center. Deleting individual Subjects from a Study
  1. In the Actions column of the record you want to delete, click Delete (the trash can icon). A message appears, prompting you to confirm deletion of the accrual count(s).
  2. Click OK. The system deletes all of the accrual counts for the site(s) you selected.
