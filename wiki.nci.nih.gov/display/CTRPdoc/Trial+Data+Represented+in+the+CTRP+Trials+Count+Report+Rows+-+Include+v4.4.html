In the CTRP Trials Count report, the system populates the trial counts (to date) in each row based on a specific trial type:
Trial Attribute Description
NCI Managed-CTEP Trials with a CTEP ID but not a DCP ID.
NCI Managed-DCP Trials with a DCP ID, with or without a CTEP ID.
NCI Sponsored-CTEP Trials for which the sponsor is "National Cancer Institute" with a CTEP ID but not a DCP ID.
NCI Sponsored-DCP Trials for which the sponsor is "National Cancer Institute" with a DCP ID, with or without a CTEP ID.
CCR Trials that meet both of the following criteria:
  • The Lead Organization for the trial is "NCI – Center for Cancer Research" (the organization identified in CTRP as CTEP ID NCICCR and PO ID 16108126).
  • The trial does not have any CTEP IDs or DCP IDs.
Center Trials-Center Registered Complete Trials with all data elements that meet both of the following criteria:
  • The trial was not submitted by CTRO or by DCP PIO.
  • The trial does not have any CTEP IDs or DCP IDs.
Center Trials-Center Registered Abbreviated Trials with limited data elements that meet both of the following criteria:
  • The trial was not submitted by CTRO or by DCP PIO.
  • The trial does not have any CTEP IDs or DCP IDs.
Industrial and Abbreviated terms are used interchangeably.
Import from Clinicaltrials.gov Trials imported from Clinicaltrials.gov.
Consortia/Other Trials with mixed characteristics of Complete and Abbreviated Trials.
The following diagram illustrates the relationships between the different trial types. The diagram is divided in two parts horizontally. The top section comprises Complete trials; the bottom section comprises Abbreviated trials. Trial types and their relationships are represented as follows:
  • NCI managed trials (large red oval) comprise Complete trials only.
  • NCI sponsored trials (red oval with white outline inside the NCI managed trials shape) comprise a subset of NCI managed trials only.
  • CCR trials (rectangle with black outline) comprise a subset of NCI managed, but not NCI sponsored trials. Only trials for which the Lead Organization is "NCI – Center for Cancer Research" are included in this group. This group does not include trials with CTEP or DCP IDs.
  • Consortia trials (circle with dashed outline) comprise a subset of NCI managed, but not NCI sponsored trials; and Abbreviated trials.
