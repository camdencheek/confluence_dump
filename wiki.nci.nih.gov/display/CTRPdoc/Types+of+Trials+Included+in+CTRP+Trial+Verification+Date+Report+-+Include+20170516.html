The CTRP Trial Verification Date report includes only those trials that meet the following criteria:
Trial Attribute Trials Included in the Report
Sponsor Trials where the sponsor is NCI.
ID Trials that have a CTEP and/or DCP ID. CTEP is an abbreviation of Cancer Therapy Evaluation Program. DCP is an abbreviation of Division of Cancer Prevention.
Milestone Trials where the current milestone is not Late Rejection Date.
Lead Organization Trials where the lead organization is not "NCI - Center for Cancer Research" (CCR).
Current Trial Status Trials where the current trial status is any status other than the following statuses:
  • Complete
  • Withdrawn
  • Administratively Complete
For details, refer to Trial Status Values in the CTRP and ClinicalTrials.gov .
Verification Date Trials with a verification date older than six months.
