You can record one or more comments about any aspect of an organization or person you are curating, whether or not you make any changes to values in the record.How to Add Comments
  1. Navigate to the record of interest.
  2. In the Action column for a given record, click Curate.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the page until you see the Curator Comment(s) section. Curator Comment(s) Section
  4. Enter your comments and then click Save.The system records your comment and adds your username and a date/time stamp for reference. Curator Comments Section - Recorded Comments
