The relation between COLLECTIONS, PATIENTS, and PATIENT_RACES is that COLLECTIONS can have multiple PATIENTS, and PATIENTS can have multiple PATIENT_RACES. These relations are maintained through the use of consistent Study Identifier and Study Subject Identifier fields.Using the format table, you can determine that you must enter the Patient's Date of Birth in the sixth position in a row in the PATIENTS table as YYYYMM.
