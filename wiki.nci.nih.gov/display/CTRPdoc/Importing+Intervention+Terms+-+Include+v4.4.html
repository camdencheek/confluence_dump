  1. On the menu, click Manage NCIt Terms.The Manage NCIt Terms page displays a menu for managing intervention terms.
  2. Click Manage NCIt Intervention Terms > Import/Sync Term with NCIt. The Import New Intervention from NCIt page appears.
  3. Enter the term's NCIt identifier code, and then click Look Up.Use the NCI Thesaurus to find the term identifierIf you do not know the term's NCIt code, you can search for it in the NCIt at http://ncit.nci.nih.gov/ .The Import New Intervention From NCIt page isÂ pre-populated with the NCIt identifier, Preferred name, and synonyms.
  4. If available, enter the CDR Identifier.
  5. Optionally, select the mode of intervention, for example, drug or device, from the Cancer.gov Type and ClinicalTrials.gov Type drop-down lists.
  6. Click Import.
