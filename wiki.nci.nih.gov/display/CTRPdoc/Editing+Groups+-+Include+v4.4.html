You can edit group records as necessary.How to Edit Group Records
  1. Search for the trial of interest. For instructions, refer to Searching for Trials in PA .
  2. In the search results, click the NCI Trial Identifier link for that trial. The Trial Identification page appears.
  3. On the Trial Identification page, check out the trial. For instructions, refer to Checking In and Checking Out Trials . (This checkout step is optional for Super Abstractors.)
  4. On the Scientific Data menu, click Groups/Cohorts. The Groups page appears.
  5. In the Edit column for a given record, click the Edit icon.The Edit Group page appears.
  6. Make changes as necessary to the Label, Description, or Intervention Assignment according to instructions in Adding Groups .You can expand text fields and table cells to reveal all of their contentTo increase the size of a text field or table cell, click and drag the lower right corner handle.
  7. Click Save.
