Trial record owners can update and amend trials in CTRP Registration. The trial submitter is listed as a trial record owner by default, but in your role as an abstractor, you can assign ownership of any trial record to any user currently registered with the CTRP. You also can remove record ownership for any current trial record owner. A trial record can have multiple owners.
