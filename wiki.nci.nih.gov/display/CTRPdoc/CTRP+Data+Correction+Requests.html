  • Discrepancies identified on multi-institutional trials may require communication with the lead organization (LO) (institutional trials) or the NCI operations office for National trials (e.g., CTEP, DCP)
  • We’ve created CTRP Data Correction Request email and form templates* for Cancer Centers to leverage when contacting the CTRO NCICTRO@mail.nih.gov (first pass review and triage) and/or follow-up with another NCI-designated Cancer Center or NCI operations office representative for National trials in support of a specific discrepancy “CTRP data correction request”
  • Download, " CTRP Data Correct Request Form* " (*for spreadsheet to open, please right click and select "Open in new tab") (*Request Form 12/2019)
  • Below please find email templates "recommended content/fields" to include in your email to the CTRO NCICTRO@mail.nih.gov  for the following:
    • I = Institutional/E = Externally Peer Reviewed:  If your Cancer Center is a Participating Site, follow this email template example:
To: NCICTRO@mail.nih.gov (CTRO) or jane.doe@genericcancercenter.com (LO CTRP Site Administrator)Subject: CTRP Discrepancy / Data Correction Request --Insert Cancer Center Name--: TRIAL IDDear CTRO (or LO CTRP Site Administrator):We would like to request the following CTRP data corrections for our center:NCI IDNCT IDReporting Period (if applicable for this request)Local Trial IDParticipating SitePO IDCTRP Field/Current DataCTRP Field/Correct DataCommentsPlease let me know if you have any questions.Regards,--Signature Block--
