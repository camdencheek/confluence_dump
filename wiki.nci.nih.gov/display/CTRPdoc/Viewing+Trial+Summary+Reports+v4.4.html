Trial Summary Reports (TSRs) contain all trial details as submitted by users via Registration, and is updated with the information you abstract. You can download the reports as necessary throughout the validation and abstraction processes. Each TSR generated throughout the processing of the trial is saved with the trial record. How to Access Trial Summary Reports
  • To download the most current TSR for a trial, do one of the following:
    • In a trial record, for example, in a list of search results, click the View TSR link in the View TSR column. The link will be functional only after the trial has been accepted.
    • On the main menu, click View TSR.
    • At the top of theÂ abstraction dashboard Details page, click View TSR.
  • To access all of the TSRs associated with the trial, select the trial of interest, and on the main menu, click Trial Related Documents. All documents associated with the trial are listed, including each TSR generated throughout the life of the trial abstraction process.
