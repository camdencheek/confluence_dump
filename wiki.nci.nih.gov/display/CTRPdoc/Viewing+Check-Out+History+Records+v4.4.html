Users provide a comment each time they check in a trial in PA. These comments are saved with the trial record and are displayed on the Check-Out History page along with other check-in/check-out details.The records display the following details:
  • Type - Administrative, Scientific, or both
  • Check-Out Time - Date and time the trial record was checked out
  • Check-Out User - Name of the user who checked out the trial
  • Checked-In Time - Date and time the trial record was checked out
  • Check-In User - Name of the user who checked in the trial
  • Check-In Comment - Notes about the record or reason why the record was checked in
How to View Check-Out History Records
  1. Search for the trial of interest. (See Searching for Trials .)
  2. Click the Trial Identifier link.
  3. On the main menu, click Check-Out History. The Check-Out History page displays the check-in and check-out details, with comments for each time the trial was checked in.
