The Data Table 4 reports include only those trials that meet the following criteria:
# Trial Attribute Trials included in the reports
3 Trial Status Any trial with an open status during the reporting period. Open statuses include the following:
  • Active
  • Available
  • Enrolling by Invitation
  • Temporarily Closed to Accrual
  • Temporarily Closed to Accrual and Intervention
  • Temporarily Not Available
For information on these statuses, refer to Trial Status Values in the CTRP and ClinicalTrials.gov and Expanded Access Statuses .
4 Participating Site Status Any trial in which a member of the specified Cancer Center organization family is a participating site with an open status (as described for trial statuses)Â during the reporting period and relative to the trial status. The system considers the following criteria:
  • An organization is a member of the specified organization family on any date in the reporting period (on or after the reporting period start date and on or before the reporting period end date).
  • This organization is a participating site on a trial.
  • This trial had an open status at this site on any date in the reporting period.
If all of these criteria are true, then the system includes this trial in the report. For examples, refer to Examples of Trials Included Based on Participating Site Status .
1 Trial Processing Status All trials with any processing status other than Rejected or Submission Terminated. For information on these statuses, refer to Trial Processing Statuses .
0 Trial Type All types. For Industrial trials without a trial status, the system assumes that the trial is ongoing.
2 Cancer Center Family All trials in which a member of the specified Cancer Center organization family (listed in CTRP as effective within the reporting period) is a participating site.
In addition, some trials have been excluded manually. For information, contact ctrp_support@nih.gov .
