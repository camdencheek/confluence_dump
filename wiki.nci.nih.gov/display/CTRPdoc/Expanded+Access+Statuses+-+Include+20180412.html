Trial status refers to the current stage or state of participant/patient enrollment in a clinical trial relative to other stages. CTRP uses the same statuses at the trial level and at the participating site level. The following table lists the valid Expanded Access trial statuses used in CTRP and in ClinicalTrials.gov. For information on other trial statuses used in CTRP and in ClinicalTrials.gov, refer to Trial Status Values in the CTRP and ClinicalTrials.gov .Current trial status definitions
