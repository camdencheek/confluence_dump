If you are a Site Administrator, you can specify the Center Principal Investigator for the trials described in Managing Data Table 4 Information for Your Center .
  1. On the toolbar, click Administration > Manage DT4 Information > Manage DT4 Information for Your Center. A page by that name appears.
  2. If you want to specify a different person, in the row for the trial of interest, in the Center Principal Investigator column, click Look Up. For instructions, refer to Looking Up Registered Persons .
This value appears in the PI columns (Last Name, First Name, Middle Initial) of the Data Table 4 report. For instructions on generating that report, refer to Running CTRP Data Table 4 Reports .
