When registering Abbreviated trials, you upload the following types of documents:
  • IRB Approval
  • Informed ConsentUpload your documents as Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx, or .docm), Adobe PDF, or WordPerfect files.
Special processing for PDF filesAdobe PDF files require special processing. Please see the information about creating PDFs in Converting Documents to PDFs .How to Submit Trial Related Documents
  1. Next to the document-type field, (e.g., IRB Approval), click Browse.
  2. Navigate to, and select, the appropriate document, and then click Open.Depending on your operating system, you may see a different command name for "Open."
  3. Repeat the steps above for each type of document.Adding Multiple "Other" DocumentsYou can upload more than one (1) "Other" document. After you have uploaded the first of your "Other" documents, click the Add More link. A new Other document field is displayed.
