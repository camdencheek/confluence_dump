Completing the Study Overall Officials Section
  1. Search for the trial of interest. For instructions, refer to Searching for Trials in PA .
  2. In the search results, select the NCI Trial Identifier link for that trial. The Trial Identification page opens.
  3. On the Trial Identification page, check out the trial. For instructions, refer to Checking In and Checking Out Trials . (This checkout step is optional for Super Abstractors.)
  4. On the Administrative Data menu, select General Trial Details. The General Trials Details page opens.
  5. On the General Trials Details page, navigate to the Study Overall Officials section. Complete the appropriate fields, see the following table for additional information. All Study Overall Officials information is optional.A Study Overall Official is a person(s) responsible for the overall scientific leadership of the protocol, including study principal investigator.
    Field Label Description/Instructions
    First Name Enter the official's first name
    Middle Name Enter the official's middle name
    Last Name Enter the official's last name
    Degree Enter the official's degree
    Organization Affiliation Enter the official's Organizational Affiliation. If none, specify Unaffiliated
    Official Role Indicate the position of function the Official. Select one of the following:
    • Study Chair
    • Study Director
    • Study Principal Investigator
  6. Select Save.
