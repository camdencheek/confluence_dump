On hold records contain information about a delay in trial processing that includes the cause of the delay, the date the delay started, and the date that the trial was taken off hold.You can continue to abstract a trial while it is on hold. However, you can not record any milestones.You can add and edit On-hold records at any time during the validation and abstraction processes. However, the only data in an On-hold record that you can edit is the Off-hold date. That is, you can take a delayed trial off hold by editing an existing On-hold record.A single trial can have one or more On-hold records at any time.For instructions, refer to the following pages:
