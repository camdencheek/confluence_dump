The CTEP PCD report includes only those trials that meet the following criteria:
Trial Attribute Trials Included in the Report
ID Trials that have a CTEP ID. CTEP is an abbreviation of Cancer Therapy Evaluation Program.
PCD Trials which meet either of the following criteria:
  • The Primary Completion Date (PCD) is about to expire (within the next 90 calendar days).
  • The PCD has expired with a date type of Anticipated.
