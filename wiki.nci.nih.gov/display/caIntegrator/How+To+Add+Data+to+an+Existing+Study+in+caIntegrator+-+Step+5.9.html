5.9. Once the query is saved, the Search page will reload and the Study Data menu in the left navigation panel will expand to show the newly saved 'Genomic Query' under the 'My Queries' heading. You can click on the magnifying glass icon to the left of the Query link to bring up the query results again, or on the pencil icon to edit the query criteria. The newly saved 'Genomic query' (highlighted in red) is shown in the 'Study Data' menu under 'My Queries'.
