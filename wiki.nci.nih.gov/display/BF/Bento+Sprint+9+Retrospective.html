What areas still need improvement?

  • List anything that we could have done better in this sprint (add your name after your comment)
  • There looks to be a discrepancy with the "Add All" button on the Explore Dashboard. I would expect this function to respect the facet filters selected by the user, such that "All" files only pertains to files in the Dashboard table, but that is not the case. We may need to revisit the original requirements. ( Kuffel, Gina (NIH/NCI) [C] )
  • Apologies for the delayed documentation tickets; they were put on hold due to other priorities. To prevent this from happening in the future, I recognize the need to define clear priorities and ensure timely delivery of work. During the planning phase, I underestimated the priority of tasks like Bento Local, which led to some delays. It's on me and work on improving our process by setting clear expectations beforehand. Let's learn from this experience and strive for better coordination and prioritization moving forward. ( Sheth, Karan (NIH/NCI) [C] )
  • Clarification on phrases like "Don't allow <user action>" - some actions cannot be prevented, so what precisely should be done in response to the action? ( Lee, Joon (NIH/NCI) [C] )

What you have learned?

  • List anything you learned about in this sprint (add your name after your comment)
  • I had a misunderstanding about how classes get passed to subcomponents. Rana, Ambar (NIH/NCI) [C] explained to me that I needed to explicitly pass the classes in places where I was not. ( Lee, Joon (NIH/NCI) [C] )
