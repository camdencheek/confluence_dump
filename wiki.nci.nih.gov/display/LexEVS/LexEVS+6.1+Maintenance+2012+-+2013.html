Summary

The goal of this project is to address JIRA items and provide maintenance and support for LexEVS 6.0. Our process is to review the LexEVS JIRA items to determine priority and status.DocumentationAdditional documentation for the project will be added throughout the lifecycle of the project. Refer also to LexEVS Guides and Training .
