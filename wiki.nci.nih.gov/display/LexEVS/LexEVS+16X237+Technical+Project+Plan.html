The purpose of this document is to document the technical project plan for the National Cancer Institute Center for Biomedical Informatics and Information Technology (NCI CBIIT) for the duration of this contract (October 1, 2016 - September 30th, 2017).

Technical Project Plan

Each week a project plan is sent to Leidos Biomedical Research, Inc. and CBIIT management. Please see the project plans below. If you have any questions please contact Craig Stancl at craig.stancl2@nih.gov .
