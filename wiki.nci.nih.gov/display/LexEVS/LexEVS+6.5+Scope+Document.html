Project Scope

The purpose of this document is to collect, analyze, and define high-level needs and features of the National Cancer Institute Center for LexEVS Release 6.5. This document focuses on the functions proposed by the product stakeholders and target users in order to make it a better product.

The objective of this release of the LexEVS is to provide a bug fixes and a set of new features as captured in the document.
