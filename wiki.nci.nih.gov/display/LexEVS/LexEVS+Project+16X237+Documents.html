Summary

The goal of this project 16X237) is to address JIRA items and provide maintenance and support for the LexEVS project and to develop and release new versions of LexEVS during the contract time period (October 1, 2016 - September 30th, 2017) with the option of 2 optional years (2018 and 2019).Additional documentation for the project will be added throughout the lifecycle of the project.

Document Purpose

The purpose of this document is to satisfy the project documents requirement to support project 16X237 for the National Cancer Institute Center for Biomedical Informatics and Information Technology (NCI CBIIT) for the duration of this contract (October 1, 2016 - September 30th, 2017 and optionally 2018 and 2019).
