Summary

The goal of this project (S13-500 MOD3) is to address JIRA items and provide maintenance and support for LexEVS 6.1 and to develop and release LexEVS 6.2.Additional documentation for the project will be added throughout the lifecycle of the project.  Refer to LexEVS 6.2 Development Documents .
