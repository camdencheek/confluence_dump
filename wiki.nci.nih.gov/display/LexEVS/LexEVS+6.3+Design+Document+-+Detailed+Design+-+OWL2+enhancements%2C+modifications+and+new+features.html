 OverviewLoading OWL2 sources is part of an ongoing project to create a viable mapping of OWL2 sources into the LexGrid model.  Challenges presented by this mapping include the punning of some owl classes into individuals and providing a mapping of annotations properties into a variety of LexEVS objects depending on the nature of the OWL2 annotation property.  In some cases drilling down into the NAry structure of boolean sentences to construct relationships that are otherwise hidden by the nature of anonymous class was necessary.  Individual examples of these issues have been detailed in the spread sheet below.
