Introduction

The Content working group is made up of representatives from the NRDS committee and additional subject matter experts nominated by NRDS Committee members.  It is co-led by a NCI and NCTN/ETCTN representative.  The purpose of the Content working group is to identify a set of CDEs for use in Rave by the NCTN and the ETCTN.  The working group will meet weekly.

NRDS caAERS Integration Content Review

Please find attached the following spreadsheets for your review and comments:
  1. Draft caAERS AE before and after recommended changes
  2. Draft caAERS SAE before and after recommended changes
  3. Draft NCTN/ETCTN Curation Style Guide
A few things to keep in mind during the review process:
  • The review period will be from Thursday August 6th, 2015 to Thursday August 20th, 2015.
    • If an extension is needed, please contact the NRDS Project Manager: Neesha Desai ( neesha.desai@nih.gov )
    • Each spreadsheet has a column to collect comments from your group, as well as a reason for the requested changes.  Please use the two columns to provide your comments.
    • Email the spreadsheets with your comments to the NRDS Project Manager:  Neesha Desai ( neesha.desai@nih.gov ) by Thursday August 20th, 2015.
    • Note remember that all comments provided must represent your organization.
  • Note to NCTN Statistical Center PIs:  Please be sure to discuss within your Stats Center the code changes being proposed to ensure the benefits outweigh any potential challenges. One change in particular to review is changing all numeric responses of ‘1’ or ‘2’ to a character response of ‘Y’ or ‘N’ (for Yes and No).  There are 30 coded indicator value sets impacted by this 1,2 to Y,N coding.  All of these changes can be found on both the caAERS AE and SAE CRF before/after spreadsheets.
