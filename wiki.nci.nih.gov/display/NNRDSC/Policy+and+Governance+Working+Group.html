Introduction

The Policy and Governance working group is made up of representatives from the NRDS committee and additional subject matter experts nominated by NRDS Committee members.  It is co-led by a NCI and NCTN/ETCTN representative.  The purpose of the Policy and Governance working group is to identify requirements to guide decisions and usage and establish principles, responsibilities, and requirements for development, maintenance and implementation of the standardized CDEs by NCTN and ETCTN.  This group will meet every 2 weeks.
