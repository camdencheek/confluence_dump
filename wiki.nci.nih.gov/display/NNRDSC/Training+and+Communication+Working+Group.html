Introduction

The Training and Communication working group is made up of representatives from the NRDS committee and additional subject matter experts nominated by NRDS Committee members.  It is co-led by a NCI and NCTN/ETCTN representative.  The purpose of the Training and Communication working group is to outline the components of a training plan and define the roles and responsibilities of NRDS Committee project participants in the review, approval and dissemination of information about key project processes, events, documents and milestones.  This group will meet every 2 weeks.
