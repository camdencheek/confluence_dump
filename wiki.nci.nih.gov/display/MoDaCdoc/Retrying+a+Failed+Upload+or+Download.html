Sometimes an upload or download fails due to network issues or other issues. For Globus transfers, consider the following steps:
  1. If you were using Globus for the transfer, check whether the Globus group has been given appropriate permissions to the target Globus endpoint. For instructions, refer to Preparing to Use Globus .
  2. View the details about a specific failed request, as described in Viewing Status .
  3. Click the retry icon in the Transfer Status column. The system submits a new request, identical to the original request.
  4. If the issue persists, select About > Contact. For details, refer to Contacting Us .
