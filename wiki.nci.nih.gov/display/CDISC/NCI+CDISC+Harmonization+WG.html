The NCI CDISC Harmonization Working Group to harmonize non-standard CDISC content into draft CDISC standards across the NCTN/ETCTN and submit to CDISC for standardization.  The working group members represent each of the NCTN/ETCTN organizations.  The working group will focus on
  • Reviewing the proposed roadmap and prioritize the content for CDISC harmonization.
  • Working collaboratively with WG members to harmonize the non-standard CDISC content across the NCTN/ETCTN.
  • Vetting the harmonized content for proposal to CDISC.
  • Providing content for CDISC harmonization.
The working group will be meeting bi-weekly to begin prioritizing the content and working on harmonization.  The deliverable of the working group include
  • Prioritized list of content for CDISC Harmonization
  • Spreadsheet of proposed CDISC content to use across the LPOs
  • Best practices used during harmonization efforts
