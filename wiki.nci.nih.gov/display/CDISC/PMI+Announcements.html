These PMI CDISC Standard Forms have been created as part of the PMI Implementation.  This is a collaborative effort between NCI CBIIT and NCI CTEP; the NCI CDISC-aligned content has been leveraged as appropriate in consideration of PMI implementation.  The PMI Standard Forms include:
  1. CDSIC harmonized question text, prompt, CDISC published CT, CDASH variables and the corresponding caDSR II Form PIDs and CDE PIDs.
  2. The variables within the CTEP standard forms have a two-letter domain prefix specified where appropriate as per our NCI CTEP CDISC implementation decision.
  3. The NCTN/ETCTN Curation Style Guide Requirements have been applied to the content in the PMI Standard Forms.
  4. The CSC DEWG Rules and Naming Conventions have been applied to the PMI Standard Forms.
If you have any questions, please send an email to pmistandards@nih.gov .A big thank you to NCI CBIIT for their collaboration and support efforts on this project, and a thank you to all of our Groups for your engagement to make this implementation a success.
