Question: Can I delete an experiment with the status as "In Progress"?

Topic: caArray Usage Release: All releases Date entered: 11/19/2010

Answer

Check your caArray version - You will not be able to delete an experiment with the status as "In Progress" if your caArray version is older than 2.3.0. Starting from version 2.3.0, we changed experiment management process to allow deletion of the experiments in the "In Progress" state. To get rid of the "In Progress" experiments in an older version, you need to upgrade your application to version 2.3.0 or newer. As of today, the current version is 2.4.0
