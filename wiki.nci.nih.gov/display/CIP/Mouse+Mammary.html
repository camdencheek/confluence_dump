Summary

The Mouse Mammary collection consists of MR images of preinvasive and invasive mammary cancer in a genetically engineered mouse model of breast cancer.  Images were acquired by the NCI’s Small Animal Imaging Program and Mouse Cancer Genetics Program.  For more information regarding the mice and images in this collection please contact Dr. Sunny Jansen ( jansensa0@gmail.com ).
