Getting Started

Welcome to the Family Cohort Registration User's Guide. This study helps the National Cancer Institute (NCI) to save lives and improve the quality of life for individuals at increased genetic risk of cancer. We conduct clinical, genomic, epidemiological and behavioral research and translate that knowledge into prevention, screening and management of cancer in families and high-risk populations.

Printing This Guide

Before we get started, if you want to print this wiki guide, follow these instructions to save it to a PDF file and then print it.
  1. Click Space Tools and select Reorder pages.
  2. Click the Export tab and select Custom Export.
  3. Select Export Formats > PDF > Next.
  4. Click Export and follow the prompts to save the file. Download and print the file.

Registration

To register yourself and additional family members to the study please follow the step-by-step registration process below:Recommended BrowsersTo avoid technical difficulties we recommend Apple users to access the system through the Safari or Firefox browsers. Windows users are recommended to access the system through the Edge or Firefox browsers.
  1. Use any laptop or tablet with internet access and navigate to https://service-stage.nci.nih.gov/charms . This will take you to the Division of Cancer Epidemiology and Genetics home page.
  2. Click on the 'Registration' option in the top left corner next to the 'Login' option.
  3. This will take the user to the "Create Your Account" page.  Please fill in all the required information (First Name, Last Name, Email Address and Phone Number). Check the box to validate that you are over 18 year's of age.
  4. Once all the required information is filled in click the 'Create Account' option at the bottom of the page.

Activation

  1. Once you create an account you will see a thank you for registering message populate on the screen. This message will also direct you to check your email to activate your Family Cohort account.
  2. Log into the email account that you used to register and you should see a new message from okta.
  3. Click on the green 'Activate NCI-External Account' option within the email message.Note: The activation link will expire, if not used within seven (7) days, and will require a new token to activate the account.
  4. A "Welcome to NCI" message will populate on the screen and request that you enter a new password. You will also be asked to choose a "Forgot my Password" question and answer. Lastly, you will be asked to choose a security image.
  5. Once you have completed filling out the required information click on the grey 'Create my Account' option at the bottom of the screen. You have now successfully activated your account and the Home Page should populate with your name in the upper right hand corner.

Two Factor Authentication

As part of our updated security measures okta will now require a cell phone number that will be used to send a verification code that must be entered in the system in addition to your username and password to gain access to the system. Once you log in the system will request that you send the code to your phone and input that number into the field before gaining access.
  1.  After the user has activated their account they will be asked to set up Multifactor Authentication. Click the 'Configure Factor' option.
  2.  The user will then be prompted to recieve a code via SMS to authenticate. Use the drop-down to pick a country. (United States in the default). Insert your cell phone number and click the 'Send Code' option.
  3. Check your text message and copy the provided code in the 'Enter Code' field then click the 'Verify' option.
  4.  The user will then be prompted to complete the process by clicking the 'Finish' option.

Log into your CHARMS NCI Account

  1. Type in your Username (email address) and Password.
  2. Click the 'Sign In' option.
  3.  Click the 'Send Code' option.
  4.  Check your text message for the provided code.
  5.  Enter the code in the 'Enter Code' box.
  6. Click the 'Verify' option.
  7.  This will take the user to the home screen. You will see your name in the upper right corner with a drop-down arrow. Click the drop-down and then click "My Family Cohort Study".

Screener Questionnaire Form

  1. Once you are logged in you should see a "Forms" link with a "Screener" icon below it. Click on the icon. This will bring you to the "Screener Questionnaire" form.
  2. This form is broken out into seven (7) sections: Study Introduction, Personal Information, Contact Information, Personal Cancer History, Family Members' Cancer History, Family Members' Genetic Tests and Final Information. There is a navigation bar on the left hand side so that you can go back-and-forth between each section. Read all of the information thoroughly and answer all questions to the best of your ability.
  3. When you complete a section click the 'Save and Next' button at the bottom of the screen. The system will automatically take you to the next section of the form. Note: The first question will ask if you are filling this questionnaire out for someone else. If the answer is yes you will be asked additional questions before going to the next section. This is also true for other questions within in the form.
  4. When the form is completed you will see checks next to each section. You will have the option to go 'Back' to a previous section, 'Save' your work, or 'Submit' your form.
