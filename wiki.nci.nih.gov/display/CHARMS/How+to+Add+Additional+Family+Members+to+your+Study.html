Adding Family Members

The Proband or Proxy are the only two participants that may add additional family members to their study. Adding family members can be critical to having a more thorough analysis of your study. Follow the steps below to add family members:
  1. From the home page click on the 'FHQ' form icon.
  2. To add a spouse or child, from the left navigation bar click the "Spouse/Partner/Child" option.
  3. Depending on which you want to add (spouse or child) click the appropriate option.
  4. Complete all fields of the form and click the 'Save' option on the bottom of the page.
  5. To add a family member whom is not a spouse or child, from the left navigation bar click on the "Family Member Information" option.
  6. Click on the 'Add Family Member' option.
  7. Complete all fields of the form and click the 'Save' option on the bottom of the page.
  8.  The additional family member(s) will receive an email notifying them they have been added to the study and informing them of the additional forms that need to be completed on their behalf.Note: If a child is under the age of 18 they will need a "Proxy" to fill out any additional forms required. Please see the Completing Enrollment Packet section for more information.
