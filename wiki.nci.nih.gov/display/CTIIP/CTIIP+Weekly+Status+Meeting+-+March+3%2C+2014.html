Discussion Summary / Decisions

  • A concern was raised about the future of NBIA and AIM, since there are no development funds for FY2014. Jose Galvez explained that while the development budget was cut due to overall funding constraints, O&M funds are still in place.  The intention is to continue to support both tools to ensure the necessary capabilities are provided to the imaging community.  While CBIIT is no longer in the "software development business", we are still tasked with ensuring the bioinformatics and IT needs of the cancer research community are met, so there will be instances where we develop a tool or bring in one that already exists.  Jose also commented that this group can participate in defining the on-going / new needs for NBIA and AIM, and can help make the case to CBIIT leadership for 2015 funding.
  • It was agreed that:
    • we should review the items that have already been documented in JIRA as new requirements for NBIA and AIM
    • we should engage a broader set of users in the field for their input as well.  There are hundreds of registered users for these tools.
  • John and Justin are helping Ulli with the SOW for small animal imaging.  We will send Dr. Holland a new draft that includes our expected scope, and will specifically indicate that we wish to use existing data for this project.  If Dr. Holland's group is unable to provide existing data, alternative researchers to contact include Brian Ross and Andy Becker.
