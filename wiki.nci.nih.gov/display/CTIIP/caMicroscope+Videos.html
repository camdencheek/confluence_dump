caMicroscope Markup Services

caMicroscope supports the creation of human markups. Users can create freeform shapes, rectangles and drop pins on areas of interest. In addition to adding geometric shapes, users can also associate annotations to markups. These annotations are structured descriptions. The form that is used to capture this description (blue box that slides in on the left) is template-driven and can be customized. The annotations and markups are managed in a customized MongoDB environment and is accessible via an API. caMicroscope Machine-Generated Annotations

caMicroscope can display machine generated annotations and markups. We provide tools and libraries that can process segmentation results and display these as overlays on the image.
