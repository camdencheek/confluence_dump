If you intend to create a link that points to a wiki page, and you intend to create that link outside of the wiki, use the Tiny Link. The Tiny Link is a permanent link. That is, it continues to work even if someone changes the page title or moves the page to a different wiki space.
  1. In the wiki page (in view mode rather than edit mode), choose one of the following methods:
    • Click the Share icon above the page title, on the right side of the page. Click Copy.
    • Click the Tools (...) icon above the page title, on the right side of the page. Select Page Information. The upper left panel of the page provides the Tiny Link. Copy the Tiny Link.
  2. In the page where you intend to create a link, paste the Tiny Link.
