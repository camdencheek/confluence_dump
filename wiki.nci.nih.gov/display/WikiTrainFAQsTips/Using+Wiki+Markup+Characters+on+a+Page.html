When you are editing a wiki page, Confluence responds to some characters with autoformatting, as described in the following Atlassian help page: https://confluence.atlassian.com/conf613/keyboard-shortcuts-964960192.html However, you can also use those characters without autoformatting.
  1. Type the character, such as an asterisk (*), or pair of characters, such as brackets ([]). Confluence responds with autoformatting.
  2. Press Ctrl+Z. Confluence cancels the autoformatting, leaving behind only what you have typed.
  3. Continue entering your text.
For example, we entered the following in this way:[This is not a  link.]
