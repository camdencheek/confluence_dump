The following are the steps to create a wiki home page for the work your team is doing in an existing wiki space.
  1. Navigate to the wiki page that is the most logical parent for your page.
  2. Add a page, as described in Adding a Page or a Blog Post .
  3. Restrict viewing or editing of the page or both if you wish, as described in Restricting Who Can View and Edit Pages .
  4. Add child pages to your wiki home page to create a page family that is a useful hierarchy of pages for your team.
  5. Consider adding a list of links that point to the child pages, as described in Putting a List of Child Pages on a Page . The left sidebar has these links, but some users hide that sidebar.
