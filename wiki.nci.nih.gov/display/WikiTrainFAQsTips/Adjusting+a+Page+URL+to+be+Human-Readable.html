When you view your wiki page, if the URL address at the top of the browser window is in the following format, you can adjust the page so that the URL is human-readable: https://wiki.nci.nih.gov/pages/viewpage.action?pageId=123456789 An example of a human-readable wiki page URL is as follows: https://wiki.nci.nih.gov/display/space/User+Guide To make your URL human-readable:
  1. Open the page for editing as described in Editing a Page .
  2. In the page title, remove special characters.
  3. When you are done editing the page, click Save.
