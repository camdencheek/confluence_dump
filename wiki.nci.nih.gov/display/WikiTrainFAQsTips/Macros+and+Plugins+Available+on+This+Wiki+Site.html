To view a list of the macros enabled on this wiki site, choose one of the following methods:
  • In the Confluence editor, type Ctrl-Shift-A.
  • Type { followed by the first few characters of the name of a macro you want to insert.
  • Choose the Insert toolbar icon > Other macros.
Macros come from the following sources:
  • Atlassian Confluence. For more information on the Confluence macros, refer to the following Atlassian help page: https://confluence.atlassian.com/conf613/macros-964960774.html
  • Various plugins. Plugins provide features of Confluence as well as macros. If you would like to request a plugin, contact NCIAppSupport@nih.gov .
Most macros and plugins are documented throughout this Wiki Tips and Guidelines space, according to the function performed by the macro or plugin.
