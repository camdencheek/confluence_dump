Description

Under the umbrella of the COPPA Project, the following four core services are being defined, scoped, designed, and built:
  • Person
  • Organization
  • Protocol Abstraction
  • Correlation
These services will be consumed by applications being built in the CTMS workspace.

Team

CTRP is a highly collaborative project made up of technical and domain experts from many organizations, including NCI, Booz Allen Hamilton, SAIC, ScenPro , 5AM Solutions , Ekagra, and SemanticBits. See the CTRP Development Team page for a list of individual team members and roles.

Email list

Subscribe to the NES-USERS-L@LIST.NIH.GOV mailing list for announcements of new releases, changes to the services, etc. You can also post to this list with any questions you may have about accessing the services. This list is intended to be fully interactive and not just for announcements from the CTRP/COPPA team - we encourage all users to ask questions and respond to anyone else's questions freely.
