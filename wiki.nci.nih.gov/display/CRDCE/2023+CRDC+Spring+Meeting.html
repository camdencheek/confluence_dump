The CRDC Spring 2023 Meeting, held on March 9 and 10, 2023, was hybrid style with both online and in-person options at NCI Shady Grove (9606 Medical Center Drive). The meeting was held to provide core CRDC community key insights into CRDC program plans and priorities, and to collaborate around component and stakeholder needs, challenges and opportunities. Participants included federal leads, key contractors and subcontractors, partners, and stakeholder groups. Meeting Agenda
