The CRDC Spring 2022 Meeting was held online on April 26 and 27, 2022. The goal of the meeting, which was intended for NIH federal staff and contractors with an interest in the CRDC, was to update the NCI and NIH community on the components, successes, and future plans for the CRDC. Topics include how researchers can utilize the CRDC to distribute data to the public and compute on data within the CRDC. Breakout sessions went into more depth on CRDC topics.
