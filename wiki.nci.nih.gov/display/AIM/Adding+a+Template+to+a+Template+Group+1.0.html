You can combine any number of templates in a template group. You may want to do this, for example, to make it easier for you to reference your data during a certain time in a longitudinal study.This procedure assumes that you have already created both templates and template groups.
  1. Click the Template Groups tab.
  2. Select the template group that you want to populate with one or more templates.
  3. In the Group Memberships panel on the right side of the screen, click the boxes next to the templates you want to add to the selected template group.
  4. Below the Group Memberships panel, click . The templates appear in the Group Members panel.
