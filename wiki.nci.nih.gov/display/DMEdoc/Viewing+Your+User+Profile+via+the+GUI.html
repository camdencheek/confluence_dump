Your user profile lists your username, DOC, default basepath, and role. If you want to change any of these attributes, contact NCIDataVault@mail.nih.gov .Your user profile also lists your permissions. To determine whether you can change these permissions, refer to Sharing Data via Permissions . To view your user profile:
  1. Log in as described in Logging In via the GUI . The Dashboard appears, with version information and your username on the right side of the banner.
  2. Click your username and select   My Profile . Your profile page appears.
